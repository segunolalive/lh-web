Registration Guideline .

Getting registered as a company, contractor, consultant or service provider with the Federal Government Lighthouse Portal is easy and simple.

  • 1

    The Lighthouse Debt Verification Database is an online system thatallows Companies registered with the CAC verify their debt status with the Federal Government.

  • 2

    To register your company on the system, you will provide your company (CAC) Registration number, FIRS (TIN), Company address details and company's contact information.

  • 3

    On successful completion, your credential (Username and Password) is sent shortly to the unique contact email address provided. Check your email for your credential (Username and Password).

  • 4

    Using your credential (Username and Password), login to the website to access information regarding your debt status to the government.

  • 5

    Based on verified company data, the company is classified by as debt free or indebted.

  • 6

    NB. No payment is required in order to be registered on the system.
